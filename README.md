# Shenzhen vs Seoul · 深圳 vs 首尔

A bilingual cinematic systems analysis of two East Asian megacities as two competing operating systems of technological civilization — speed vs precision, motherboard vs display, supply-chain Darwinism vs vertically integrated industrial OS.

> "The competition between cities is ultimately a competition between civilization architectures."
> 「城市之间的竞争，本质上是文明架构之间的竞争。」

## Structure (10 chapters + opening + coda)

1. **Philosophy** — open-source evolution vs enterprise architecture.
2. **Industrial timeline** — 1961 → 2035, parallel side-by-side year column.
3. **Supply chain** — Huaqiangbei pipeline for 5 product classes vs Korea's chaebol stack.
4. **AI · robotics** — embodied intelligence (Shenzhen) vs entertainment + on-device AI (Seoul).
5. **Culture** — motherboard civilization vs display civilization.
6. **Urban form** — velocity urbanism vs compression urbanism, with hourly-rhythm bars.
7. **Tech giants** — BYD, Tencent, Huawei, DJI, ZTE × Samsung, LG, SK Hynix, Naver, Kakao.
8. **Geopolitics** — Pacific-Rim chip-flow atlas + exposure surfaces for both cities.
9. **Civilization layer** — factories as frozen intelligence, supply chains as physical neural nets.
10. **2050 simulation** — what each OS looks like when its trajectory finishes compiling.
